About Oly’s Chai-Ghar

“TEA | SNACKS | GOSSIPS”

Our Story

Started with a passion for chai and a dream to create homely moments, Oly’s Chai-Ghar has grown into a soulful space offering 100+ varieties of teas served in traditional kullhads.

Satpal Arora, Founder – Olys Chai Ghar "I started Olys Chai Ghar with one belief — a cup of tea can spark conversations, build bonds, and inspire dreams. Today, it’s more than tea — it’s a place where Tea, Snacks & Gossips come alive. Join us Let’s build a chai culture the world remembers.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To make Olys Chai Ghar a global symbol of warmth, friendship, and everyday joy — where one cup of tea can start a story, spark a dream, or build a future.

Mission

To craft unforgettable tea experiences that feel like home — no matter where you are in the world. To inspire entrepreneurship through our franchise model, making success accessible to every chai lover. To turn “Tea. Snacks. Gossips.” into a movement — a lifestyle that brings people together, one sip at a time.
